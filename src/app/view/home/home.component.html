The Best Travel Agency

The Municipal District of Capachica offers the best travel agency to discover the magic of Lake Titicaca with unique and authentic experiences. Through exclusive tours, you can explore the floating and natural islands, such as Amantaní and Tikonata, with local guides who will immerse you in the region’s culture and ancestral traditions. Enjoy experiential tourism, sharing the hospitality of Quechua communities, their gastronomy, and way of life. Our routes will take you to breathtaking landscapes, scenic hikes, and boat rides at sunrise, allowing you to connect with nature at its finest. Experience an unforgettable adventure with the best travel agency in Capachica and let the charm of Titicaca turn your journey into a once-in-a-lifetime experience.
